Our GOOD. IT

Our core GOOD value gives us a strong sense of stewardship to the communities surrounding all of our production hubs. We see an enormous opportunity to foster the potential and talent of the youth around us by opening the door to a future they otherwise could not imagine.

{WeCanCode} WCC Hack

We have deployed the global platform WeCanCode to organize and run free hackathon events in Morocco, Ensenada Mexico, Casablanca, and Tijuana; we are teaching newcomers to crack code.

Our WCC hackathon events offer contests on a theme - areas of focus have included government, education, water conservation, and sponsored challenges. Hackers will also have the option to take on their own projects and use their imagination to develop a project with no restrictions. The hackathons culminate with our participants presenting their solutions to a range of invited guests who then vote on the best solution to a problem - there are winners!

Hunger to Learn:
Advancio Answers the Call

Observing the intense desire to learn in our Hackathons participants opened our eyes to a need that we were called to answer. It was obvious we needed to teach in a more structured way and so the Advancio Academy was born.
