Our onshore consulting services provide our clients access to expertise not available in-house such as advisory solutions and systems architecture, IT Support, UX Design, and Security Assessment.

Our nearshore model works very well for customers who are looking to have strong control over their resources and closely manage their day-to-day progress and efficiency.

This model keeps our team within 3 times zones of a client’s location, providing quality talent, fast response times, and similar cultures. Additionally, for our clients based in North America, the NAFTA free trade agreement allows for relaxed travel restrictions, legal protections, and intellectual property protections.

Our offshore model complements our nearshore outsourcing offering, allowing us to be available globally and provide clients with continuous production. Our 2 hubs also allow us to serve as both onshore and offshore development centers depending on our client’s needs.

In the virtual captives model, your company will leverage our resources and local presence to establish a custom-built outsourcing model. This product is generally offered to organizations looking to outsource all or large portion of their IT staffing. It is ideal for mid to large size organizations looking to cut cost and not sacrifice control or quality.

This type of outsourcing is particularly popular for our clients who have dedicated on staff personnel and that are looking to expand their current IT department without all the hassle of having to open a shared service or nearshore location themselves but want to contain their IT budget.

We will set up dedicated production centers according to the specifications of the client. Location, talent, size, scalability all set up with the client in mind.

We limit the risk of outsourcing your team to another state, country, and/or continent. But, keeping you in control of all the details. Generally, this model is great for seasoned outsourcing organizations that are looking to invest in their infrastrustructure.
