Advancio provides an onShore development model. This type of service has been popular, especially among IT departments experiencing an immediate need or periodic demand in qualified and skilled software developers, analysts, and testers at short notice. Advancio's specialists have been involved in large scale projects in insurance, public health, banking, currency trading, retail, and pharmaceuticals vertical markets.

The onShore model is talent that is deployed from our client home country to our local location. Advancio encourages customers to participate in selecting and/or approving a team of Advancio’s software engineers that will work for them. The engineers are carefully pre-screened according to the customer’s requirements (technical and communication skills, experience level and profession).

The team is deployed for as long as necessary to successfully complete the project (six months on average) or on an ongoing basis if the customer chooses to re-use the team on new related projects. Advancio specialists work together with, or more often as a part of, the customer’s project or product team, thus becoming closely familiar with customer's trade and congruent with customer's short-term, mid-term, and long-term business objectives.

Nearshoring is defined as service offerings offered by a foreign country that is relatively close to the home base of the company requesting the service and offers geographic, temporal (time zone), cultural, linguistic, economic, political, or historical linkage. The concept of nearshore has gained momentum in the past five years, most providers began offering it as option to their clients as an alternative to offshore outsourcing.

Our nearshore model works very well for our European, Canadian, or American customers that are looking to have strong control over their resources and closely manage their day to day progress and efficiency.

With resources within 3 timezones of the clients location, nearshoring provides clients with fast response, similar cultures and quality talent. Additionally, for our clients based in USA and/or Canada, the NAFTA free trade agreement allows for relaxed travel restrictions, Legal protection, and Intellectual property protection.

This product is optimal for organizations with previous offshoring experience and/or limited IT budgets that are looking for something better.In addition to our global locations we have partners strategically located around the world. Our partners undergo a rigorous quality assurance and project management training that insure the best possible quality and high yield on investment.

They have an advanced understanding of the american culture. We additionally restructured our internal processing model to have all of our IT Offshore contracts have their assigned Business Analyst and Project Manager in the production center that would be considered nearshore to their organization. This eliminates cultural barriers that might be represented in design and/or communication.

In addition to our production center we also have partner locations. We have worked diligently with our partner to streamline processing, eliminate communication errors, and increase efficiency. We quantify and monitor the progress through our internally used CRM system that allows all stakeholders to view and edit minute-to-minute changes and request.

We have sat on the receiving end and came to realize that the way outsourcing is currently offered by our competitors was more of a problem then a viable solution. We made it our goal to improve the process, what you see is not what you get here. The result is superior customer service and a quality product at a fraction of the cost.

In the virtual captives model, your company will leverage our resources and local presence to establish a custom-built outsourcing model. This product is generally offered to organizations looking to outsource all or large portion of their IT staffing. It is ideal for mid to large size organizations looking to cut cost and not sacrifice control or quality.

This type of outsourcing is particularly popular for our clients who have dedicated on staff personnel and that are looking to expand their current IT department without all the hassle of having to open a shared service or nearshore location themselves but want to contain their IT budget.

We will set up dedicated production centers according to the specifications of the client. Location, talent, size, scalability all set up with the client in mind.

We limit the risk of outsourcing your team to another state, country, and/or continent. But, keeping you in control of all the details. Generally, this model is great for seasoned outsourcing organizations that are looking to invest in their infrastrustructure.
